## Hero

Move fingers, eyes, mouth — even drumming — naturally, with an ordinary webcam.

## Subcopy

Start 2D/3D avatar streaming without expensive gear. KGM1 is a tracking foundation for streamers that combines local inference, stabilization, skeletal constraints, and a low-latency protocol.

## Feature sections

### Track every finger individually

Handles MCP/PIP/DIP/Tip per finger and outputs curl, spread, pinch, contact, and velocity.

### Eyes and mouth, precisely

Stabilizes blinks, gaze, iris, mouth open, mouth width, lip pucker, smile, and vowel-like mouth shapes.

### Track drum performance too

Fuses stick trajectory, hit position, hit velocity, audio onset, and foot pedals, converting the performance into avatar expression.

### Prevent weird motion

One Euro Filter, outlier rejection, skeletal constraints, velocity limits, and occlusion recovery prevent jitter, broken joints, and warping.
